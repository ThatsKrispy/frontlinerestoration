Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to digital inclusion

Our Commitment

Frontline Restoration LLC is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually improve the user experience for everyone and apply relevant accessibility standards. We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA.

Measures Taken

To support accessibility, Frontline Restoration has implemented the following measures:

  • Semantic HTML structure for screen reader compatibility
  • ARIA labels and roles throughout the site
  • Skip navigation link for keyboard users
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios (minimum 4.5:1 for normal text)
  • Keyboard navigable interface with visible focus indicators
  • Descriptive alt text for all meaningful images
  • Form labels associated with their controls
  • Responsive design that works at 200% zoom
  • Respect for user's reduced motion preference
  • No flashing content that could trigger seizures

Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, some older content or third-party integrations (such as embedded maps) may not fully meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards. We are working to improve these areas. For critical information available on maps, please contact us directly.
